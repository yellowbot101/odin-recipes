How to Make an Awesome Cheese Pizza!

Description

A cheese pizza is a classic recipe that can be done at home with little effort. It can be a fun project to do with family or a creative little date!

Do not feel the need to follow this recipe to its exact specification. Add whatever is your favorite cheese, toppings, or any additional seasoning. Make the pizza your own!

Ingredients

  • Cheese of choice
  • Tomato Sauce of choice
  • Dough of choice

If you want to mimic our recipe, we used prepackaged, store-bought dough; a jar of Prego Traditional; and a mix of mozzarella, parmesan, and gouda cheese.

Steps

Now, the real fun: how the hell do you make it?? Luckily, the actual process is fairly easy. Just follow along!

  1. Preheat the oven to 475 degrees F. (or 245 in C)
  2. Get your dough and start rolling. You'll want to do your best to make yourself a little circle. Sometimes, people will throw it up in the air like some wizard. We suck at that and instead manually roll it out and then curl the edges to make the crust.
  3. Sauce that boy! You'll want to try and make an even spread of the sauce for the most optimal coverage.
  4. Cheese it up! As they say, "this is where the fun begins." Start dropping on that cheese till you feel satisfied. It is probably better to do more cheese than less.
  5. Start cooking. It should take only 10-15 minutes to cook, so you're in luck if you're hungry.
  6. Let rest and then enjoy!
