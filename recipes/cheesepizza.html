How to Make an Awesome Cheese Pizza!

Description

A cheese pizza is a classic recipe that can be done at home with little effort. It can be a fun project to do with family or a creative little date!

Do not feel the need to follow this recipe to its exact specification. Add whatever is your favorite cheese, toppings, or any additional seasoning. Make the pizza your own!

Ingredients

  • Cheese of choice
  • Tomato Sauce of choice
  • Dough of choice
